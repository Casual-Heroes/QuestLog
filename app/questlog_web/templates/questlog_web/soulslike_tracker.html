What it does

Auto death detection

Reads the YOU DIED screen automatically. No button pressing.

Boss checklist

Every boss organised by area. Tick them off as you go.

Rage Index

Builds with deaths, decays with kills. Go hollow enough times and it shows.

OBS overlay

Browser source that updates live. Perfect for streaming.

Multiple runs

Create separate runs for each playthrough. Everything saves between sessions.

QuestLog sync

Your runs sync to your QuestLog profile. Leaderboards coming soon.
