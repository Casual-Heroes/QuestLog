QuestLog Network Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 2026

The QuestLog Network connects gaming communities across Discord and Fluxer under one roof. To keep the network a welcoming space for all gamers, every community listed in the directory must follow these guidelines. Failure to do so may result in your community being suspended or permanently banned from the QuestLog Network.

1 Be a Real Community

  • Your community must be active. No placeholder, bot farm, or spam servers.
  • Your server description must accurately represent what your community is about.
  • Invite links must lead to the server you registered. Do not bait-and-switch.

2 No Hate, Harassment, or Discrimination

  • Communities that promote, tolerate, or center around hate speech, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or discrimination of any kind are not permitted.
  • Harassment, doxxing, targeted abuse, or coordinated attacks on individuals or groups are grounds for immediate permanent removal.
  • Slurs in server names, descriptions, icons, or banners will result in immediate removal.

3 No NSFW or Illegal Content

  • NSFW content of any kind in your community listing (name, description, icon, banner, links) will result in automatic removal.
  • Communities that facilitate piracy, cheating, modding for competitive advantage, or illegal activity are not welcome.
  • No malware, phishing links, or scam invite bait.

4 Respect the Network

  • Community owners are responsible for the conduct of their moderators and members as it relates to the QuestLog Network.
  • Do not abuse QuestLog XP, Hero Points, or LFG systems. Exploit attempts will result in removal and a ban.
  • Do not register fake or duplicate communities to game the directory ranking.
  • Do not use your QuestLog Network listing to advertise competing platforms or services in a deceptive way.

5 Keep Your Listing Accurate

  • If your server closes or goes inactive, remove it from the directory or let us know.
  • Tags and descriptions must reflect your actual community - misleading tags to gain visibility are not allowed.
  • Keep your invite links working. Broken or expired links will result in your listing being hidden.

6 Leaving and Rejoining the Network

  • You can voluntarily leave the QuestLog Network at any time from your community registration page.
  • If you leave, you may rejoin without reapplying as long as you return within 90 days.
  • After 90 days, you must submit a new application and go through the review process again.
  • Communities removed by admins for guideline violations do not qualify for the 90-day rejoin window.

Enforcement

QuestLog Network admins review all community applications before approval. Approved communities can still be reported and reviewed at any time. Depending on the severity of the violation, enforcement actions range from a warning and listing edit request, to suspension, to permanent removal and ban from the network.

Severe violations (CSAM, doxxing, illegal activity) result in an immediate permanent ban with no appeal. For everything else, we aim to give community owners a chance to correct the issue first.
