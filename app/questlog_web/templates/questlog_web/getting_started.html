Welcome to Casual Heroes

Getting Started with QuestLog

QuestLog is the web platform for the Casual Heroes community. This guide covers everything you need to know to get set up and get the most out of it.

1

Create Your Account

Head to questlog.casual-heroes.com/register and sign up with a username, email, and password. You'll get a verification email - click the link to activate your account.

2

Join Our Fluxer Server

Fluxer is our primary community hub - voice, text, LFG, events, and more. Join at fluxer.gg/zplS0MwI and then link your Fluxer account inside QuestLog under your profile settings. Linking unlocks the full member portal experience.

Member Portal LFG on Fluxer Flairs & XP Events & Giveaways
3

Link Your Steam Account

Go to your profile settings and connect Steam. This unlocks SteamQuest - browse your entire library, find hidden gems, see what games you share with other members, and track playtime.

Steam is optional but highly recommended. Your library stays private unless you set it to public in Steam.

4

Set Up Your Profile

Your public profile is your gamer identity on QuestLog. Add an avatar, bio, your favorite games and genres, your platforms, and your playstyle. Other members find groups and follow people based on this.

Avatar & Banner Favorite Games Platforms Playstyle

What's Available

Everything on QuestLog

Looking for Group (LFG)

Post or join groups for any game. Filter by game, platform, experience level, and server type. Works cross-platform - LFG posts sync between QuestLog and Fluxer.

Start a Group Browse Groups

Social Feed

Share gaming moments, clips, screenshots, and thoughts. Follow members you like. No algorithm - you see posts from people you follow, in order.

Go to Feed

SteamQuest

Your full Steam library, organized. Discover unplayed games, filter by genre and tags, see who else in the community owns the same games, and find your next session.

Open SteamQuest

Communities

Browse gaming communities across Fluxer, Discord, and Matrix. Find servers that match your games and vibe, or list your own community for free.

Browse Communities List Your Community

Legacy & XP

Earn XP for everything you do - posting, grouping, commenting, logging in. Level up through Legacy tiers. Your rank is a reflection of your time and contribution to the community, not your wallet.

See Legacy Tiers
