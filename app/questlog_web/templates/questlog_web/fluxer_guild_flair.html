Flair Management

Customize flair names, costs, and availability for your server

Flairs are cosmetic badges that members can equip. Enable or disable flairs for your server, adjust HP costs, and create custom flairs unique to your community. Changes apply to all new purchases in your Fluxer guild.

Flair Role Sync

When enabled, the bot automatically creates and assigns a Fluxer role (e.g. "Flair: 🎮 Gamer") when a member equips a flair. Disable to keep your role list clean.

To display flair roles above other roles, go to your server's Role settings and drag flair roles to the top of the list manually.
