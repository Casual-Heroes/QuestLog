How YouTube Integration Will Work

1

OAuth Connection: Users will connect their YouTube channels through a secure Google OAuth flow on the Casual Heroes website.

2

API Monitoring: The bot will poll YouTube Data API for new uploads, live streams, premieres, and community posts from linked channels.

3

Rich Embeds: Videos will be announced with beautiful embeds showing thumbnails, titles, descriptions, and direct links.

4

Milestone Tracking: Set subscriber goals and the bot will automatically celebrate when creators reach them with custom messages.
