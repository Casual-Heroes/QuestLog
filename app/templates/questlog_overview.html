Modular by Design

Keep the free core or unlock only what you need — up to the Complete Suite.

  • Engagement — XP/levels, flairs, profiles, level-up embeds, premium XP Boost Events.
  • Roles — Reaction roles, role/channel templates, bulk role tools.
  • Moderation & Security — Warnings/timeouts/bans, audit exports, verification, anti-raid posture.
  • Discovery — Creator/Game discovery, Found Games dashboard, IGDB filters, announcement caps.
  • Events & Attendance — LFG, attendance history, reliability/blacklist controls.
  • Complete Suite — All modules at a bundled price (see billing page).
  • Free Core — Base XP, basic templates, reaction menus, welcome/level-up messaging.

Security First

  • Dashboard requires Discord auth and proper server permissions.
  • Module enable/disable switches are enforced across logging and automations.
  • Audit exports and destructive actions are limited to authorized admins.
  • Reaction roles and templates honor Discord role hierarchy constraints.
