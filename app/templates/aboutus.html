About Casual Heroes

A gaming community built by adults, for adults. No grind mentality, no toxicity, no drama. Just real people who love good games and good company. We run our own game servers, our own community platform (QuestLog), and our own self-hosted voice and chat powered by Matrix. We are here to play games together, have fun, and maybe make some friends along the way.

Who We Are

Casual Heroes is a tight-knit community of gamers, parents, and working adults who take games seriously without taking themselves too seriously. We get that life comes first. Sessions get cut short, schedules are unpredictable, and you are not here to be judged for it.

What we have built here is not a Discord server with a bot and a few channels. It is a full community infrastructure: dedicated game servers, our own community platform (QuestLog), self-hosted voice and chat, and a group of people who genuinely give a damn.

What We Are Building

  • QuestLog - Our custom community platform. Social profiles, game discovery, server voting, and more. Built in-house, self-hosted, no algorithm.
  • Community Game Servers - Dedicated servers rotated by community vote. Current and past rotations include V Rising, 7 Days to Die, and Valheim.
  • Self-Hosted Infrastructure - Voice, chat, and community tools we run ourselves. No third-party surveillance, no ads, no data harvesting.
  • A Space That Respects Your Time - Play when you can, at your own pace. No dailies, no streaks, no pressure.

If that sounds like your kind of community, come find out for yourself.
