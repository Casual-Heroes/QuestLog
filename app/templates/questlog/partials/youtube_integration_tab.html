How YouTube Integration Works

1

Creators connect their YouTube channels via Featured Creators page using secure Google OAuth

2

Admins approve which creators can trigger stream notifications (prevents spam)

3

Bot checks every 3 minutes if approved streamers are live on YouTube

4

When a streamer goes live, a rich notification with thumbnail, title, and viewer count is posted to your notification channel
