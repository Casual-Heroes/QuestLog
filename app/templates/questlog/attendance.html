How Attendance & Blacklist Works

1

Update Attendance Status

Use the dropdown next to each member's name to update their status: Showed (✅), No-Show (❌), Late (⏰), Cancelled (🚫), Confirmed (📋), or Pardoned (🙏)

2

Reliability Scores

Reliability is calculated from Showed (100%), Late (80%), and No-Show (0%). Pardoned and Cancelled don't count - they're excluded from the score entirely.

3

Auto-Blacklist System

Members are auto-blacklisted when they reach the configured no-show threshold (e.g., 3 no-shows). Pardoning a no-show reduces the count and can automatically remove the blacklist.

4

Manual Blacklist

Click the 🚫 Ban button to manually blacklist a member. This prevents them from joining ALL future LFG groups server-wide (not just one group).

5

Unblacklist Members

Click the ✓ Unban button on blacklisted members to restore their access. Auto-blacklisted members will be automatically unblacklisted if their no-show count drops below the threshold.

6

Group Pardon

Click the 🙏 Pardon button next to a member to pardon ONLY that specific no-show for THIS GROUP. Reduces their total no-show count by 1 and may remove from blacklist if they drop below threshold.

7

GLOBAL PARDON (Fresh Start)

Use the Grant Global Pardon button in the sidebar to give a member a complete fresh start across ALL LFG groups. This RESETS all counters to 0, resets reliability to 100%, and removes from blacklist. Historical records are kept. They can be auto-blacklisted again if new no-shows occur.

8

Bulk Updates

Click Bulk Update on any group to quickly update multiple members at once. Use Quick Actions or set individual statuses before saving.

Important Notes:

  • Pardoned Status (🙏): Valid excuse for single event (sick, emergency) - removes no-show penalty without adding to reliability
  • Group Pardon (🙏 Pardon button): Pardons THIS GROUP ONLY - reduces total no-show count by 1, may remove from blacklist if below threshold
  • GLOBAL PARDON (Sidebar): Complete fresh start across ALL groups - RESETS all counters to 0, reliability to 100%, removes from blacklist. Historical records kept.
  • Cancelled (🚫): Member cancelled in advance - doesn't affect reliability or no-show count
  • Blacklist Scope: All blacklists (auto & manual) are server-wide - affects ALL LFG groups, not just one
  • Auto-Removal: Only auto-blacklists are removed automatically when pardoned - manual blacklists require manual unban
