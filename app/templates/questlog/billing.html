Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! All subscriptions are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from this page.

What happens to my data if I unsubscribe?

Your data is never deleted! All your XP, settings, and configurations are safely stored. Premium features are disabled, but you can resubscribe anytime to instantly regain access.

Can I switch between modules?

Absolutely! Add or remove modules at any time. Changes are prorated to your billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked.
