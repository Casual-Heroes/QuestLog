What We're Running

PC-NA - from World Bosses to Trials, we do it all at a pace that respects your life.

  • World Bosses: Open-world boss hunts, sets, and group farming
  • Raids (Trials): Learning runs, no parse culture, no toxic wipes
  • Dungeons: Normal and vet runs, all skill levels welcome
  • Pledges: Daily dungeon pledges for keys and undaunted ranks
  • PvP: Battlegrounds and Cyrodiil nights when people are feeling it
  • And More: Leveling help, build advice, crafting writs, housing tours

Who We're Looking For

All classes welcome. All skill levels welcome. Just bring the vibes.

  • New & Returning Players: If you're learning the game, you belong here. We'll figure it out together.
  • Patient Healers: Down to heal dungeons and help newer players learn? You're gold.
  • Tanks: Dungeon tanks now. Trial tanks later. New or experienced both welcome.
  • Crafters: Help with gear, traits, food, potions - the guild backbone.
  • Adults with Jobs: We run content when we can, not when we must. Real life always comes first.
