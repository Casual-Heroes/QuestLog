Casual Heroes in ESO

Casual Heroes is a guild built for adults who want to experience ESO — from the ground up — without the expectations and burnout of a hardcore guild. Most of us are 20+ with jobs, families, and real-life responsibilities.

Some of us are returning players. Some of us are brand new. Either way, we’re here for the same thing: a crew to learn with, run content with, and actually enjoy the game with.

We want to grow into dungeons, Trials, and PvP — we’re just doing it the right way: learning mechanics, building solid groups, and progressing at a pace that makes sense.

Right now we’re focused on:

  • Leveling and gearing
  • Dungeon runs (normal → veteran when ready)
  • Crafting, builds, and “what the hell do I do next?” help
  • Battlegrounds / Cyrodiil nights when people are feeling it

We’re not locked into a sweaty schedule. We organize runs when people are online, and we’ll build consistent nights as the roster grows.

If you want a chill, no-drama guild where you can learn, improve, and push harder content over time — you’ll fit in here.

What We’re Running

  • Dungeons: Normal runs while we level — vet when we’re ready
  • Trials: Learning runs as the roster grows (no toxic parse culture)
  • PvP Nights: Battlegrounds and Cyrodiil when people are feeling it
  • Build & Gear Help: Sets, traits, mundus, CP paths — we’ll help you get dialed in
  • Leveling & Story: Zone clears, skyshards + skill points, new-character help
  • Crafting & Gold: Writs, farming routes, motifs/materials
  • Casual First: Flexible schedules, late-night squads, no pressure to grind

Guild Roles & What We Need

PC-NA - We’re always welcoming friendly players. Here’s what helps most as we build from scratch:

  • New & Returning Players: If you’re learning the game, you belong here. We’ll figure it out together.
  • Patient Healers: If you’re down to heal dungeons and help newer players learn, you’re gold.
  • Tanks (New or Experienced): Dungeon tanks now. Trial tanks later as we ramp up.
  • Dungeon / Trial Guides: Returning vets who can explain mechanics and be patient as we learn.
  • Crafters: People who can help with gear, traits, food/pots, and “what should I make?” questions.
  • PvP Leads: Anyone who enjoys organizing BGs/Cyrodiil nights when the mood hits.

Jump In Anytime

There’s no rush, we play at our own pace. Just bring the vibes and we’ll take care of the rest.
